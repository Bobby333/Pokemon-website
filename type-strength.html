How to Use this

When constructing a team do your best to never repeat types so that your team has a wide scale of typing coverage cause if your team is primarily fire then one water type would be able to take most of your team out but if you have a team that has both fire and electric then you can kill that water type with the electric type and this goes for a whole team of Pokemon try your best to have a very varied team if need be though you can use a Pokemon that has a secondary type the same as another Pokemon cause the primary type is still different giving type coverage.
